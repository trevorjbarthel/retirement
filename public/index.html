Concurrent Receipt Rules

If your VA disability rating is below 50%, your retirement pay may be reduced by the amount of VA compensation (VA waiver). However, if you qualify for CRDP (Concurrent Retirement and Disability Pay) at 50% or higher VA rating, you can receive both full retirement pay AND full VA compensation with no offset. Members with combat-related disabilities may qualify for CRSC regardless of rating percentage. Consult your finance office for your specific situation.
